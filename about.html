About EU Tender Match

EU Tender Match helps companies find public procurement opportunities that actually fit their business.

Many companies know that European public procurement can create serious growth opportunities, but the search process is time-consuming. Notices can be long, technical, multilingual, and difficult to filter. Our service turns that search into a clear, ranked tender-fit report.

What we believe

Companies do not need more raw tender lists. They need relevant opportunities, explained clearly, with enough context to decide whether to bid.

What we do

We monitor public procurement notices, map opportunities to your company profile, and provide summaries, fit scores, deadlines, CPV information, risk notes, and recommended next steps.

EU Tender Match is independent. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by TED, SIMAP, the Publications Office of the European Union, or any EU institution.

Who we support

We support SMEs, exporters, consultants, software companies, engineering firms, facility service providers, logistics suppliers, medical suppliers, and other businesses looking for suitable public tender opportunities in Europe.

EU Tender Match helps companies find EU public procurement opportunities that match their services, countries, CPV codes, and bidding capacity.
