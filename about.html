About EU Tender Match

EU Tender Match helps companies receive TED tenders that match their business needs.

Many companies want to sell to public buyers in Europe but do not have time to search tender portals manually. Notices can be long, technical, multilingual, and hard to filter. Our service turns public procurement search into a clear email report.

What we do

We search TED public procurement notices, filter irrelevant results, and email a Tender Match Report containing opportunities that appear relevant to your company profile, services, countries, CPV codes, contract preferences, and bidding capacity.

Who it is for

EU Tender Match is designed for SMEs, exporters, consultants, IT companies, engineering firms, facility service providers, logistics suppliers, medical suppliers, and other businesses that want better visibility of European tender opportunities.

Launch offer

As a startup, we are offering the first week free to a limited number of founding customers. In return, we may ask for feedback so we can improve the quality of the matching and the report format.

EU Tender Match is independent. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by TED, SIMAP, the Publications Office of the European Union, the European Union, or any contracting authority.
