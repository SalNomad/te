How it works

Send an inquiry. We build your tender profile. You receive matched reports.

Inquiry

Send your company name, location, services, target countries, markets, and tender interests.

Profile

We set up a tender profile using sectors, CPV codes, keywords, exclusions, and markets.

Match

You receive reports by email with the tenders that appear most relevant to your company.
