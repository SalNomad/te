EU Tender Match helps companies understand public tender opportunities by transforming procurement notices into practical business reports.

Our data sources

Our reports may use public procurement data from official sources, including Tenders Electronic Daily and other procurement portals. We identify source links where practical so customers can verify the original notice and documents.

Our added value

EU Tender Match adds independent analysis, including tender summaries, matching logic, fit scores, deadline risk, eligibility notes, CPV interpretation, country and sector filters, and bid/no-bid recommendations.

What customers must verify

  • Official tender notice and procurement documents.
  • Submission deadline and submission portal.
  • Eligibility rules, certificates, and technical specifications.
  • Contract value, language requirements, clarifications, and amendments.

No guarantee of winning

A high fit score does not guarantee eligibility, acceptance, shortlist selection, or contract award. It means the opportunity appears commercially relevant based on available data and the customer profile.

Independence

EU Tender Match is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by TED, SIMAP, the Publications Office of the European Union, the European Union, or any contracting authority.
