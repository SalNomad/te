Frequently asked questions

Do you submit bids for us?

Not by default. Our core service is tender discovery, matching, reporting, and bid/no-bid support. Bid writing can be offered separately by agreement.

Do you guarantee that we will win a tender?

No. We help you find and understand relevant opportunities. Winning depends on eligibility, tender documents, competition, pricing, technical quality, and buyer evaluation.

Can we change our company profile?

Yes. Updating the profile improves matching quality. Send updated services, countries, keywords, CPV codes, exclusions, and contract preferences.

Where should we verify official tender details?

Always verify deadlines, documents, clarifications, and submission instructions on the original official procurement source before bidding.

EU Tender Match helps companies find EU public procurement opportunities that match their services, countries, CPV codes, and bidding capacity.
