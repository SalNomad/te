Frequently asked questions

What is included in the free first week?

Selected founding customers can receive matched TED tender reports by email for the first week, subject to availability. After the free week, customers can choose a paid 30-day plan. There is no automatic paid subscription unless confirmed.

Do you submit bids for us?

Not by default. Our core service is tender discovery, matching, reporting, and bid/no-bid visibility. Bid writing or bid management may be offered separately by written agreement.

Do I need CPV codes?

No. If you already know your CPV codes, include them in the inquiry. If not, describe your services and markets, and we can build a starting profile.

How often can I receive reports?

You can request daily, weekly, or monthly emailed reports. Daily is best for frequent updates; weekly is best for most SMEs; monthly is best for a simple low-volume check.

Where should official tender details be verified?

Always verify deadlines, documents, clarifications, and submission instructions on the original official procurement source before bidding.
